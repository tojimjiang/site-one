“Ancient” UI Stuff ¬∂

Just some of my earlier work that was more self contained and didn’t really bother others. Mostly just to show the flexibility and responsiveness of well designed JavaScript.
Word Based Clock You don’t need to buy a word clock, when you can just emulate it in your browser with Local User Time and some JS magic to stay in sync and beautiful. Currently only available in dark mode. hosted on static GH Pages

Dot Matrix LED Emulator Why spend time soldering LEDs when you can just use your browser to just emulate a dot-matrix LED display in full RGB glory. Create your custom colors and designs or just use Text-to-Display for some quick dot-matric messages. hosted on static GH Pages

Color Mini-Canvas Why let yourself or home become a mess with paint? Create your custom colors and designs in the digital realm on this ephemeral web page “canvas” and forget the mess. hosted on static GH Pages

Candy Matching Actually not a ripoff of Candy Crush, more just a flip card game using candy as the playing medium. Its a bit loud [turn down your speakers OR mute the program (mute available on the page!)]. You can save your score to your local browser if you so desire. hosted on static GH Pages

Definitely Not Evil Dice Roller Why bother wasting real money when your going to get hustled anyway? Play from the comfort in your own home, without losing any actual money on this definitely not rigged dice game (it’s rigged.) hosted on static GH Pages
